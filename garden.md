# KRV Bridge Connection Community Garden
### In Collaboration with the KRV Garden Club

The **KRV Bridge Connection Community Garden** is a multipurpose space dedicated to sustainability, education, and community support. By combining the expertise of the **KRV Garden Club** with local volunteer efforts, we cultivate a space that nourishes both the land and our neighbors.

---

## 🌽 Growing for the Community
Our primary mission is to provide high-quality, fresh produce to the **Emergency Choice Pantry**. By growing food locally, we ensure that those utilizing the pantry have access to fresh supplemental nutrition that is often difficult to source.

<img src="{{ images.produce.url }}" width="{{ images.produce.width }}" height="{{ images.produce.height }}" alt="{{ images.produce.alt }}" loading="lazy" class="block full-width" crossorigin="anonymous" referrerpolicy="no-referrer" />

## 📚 Education & Growth
The garden serves as a living classroom. We offer hands-on educational opportunities for:
* **Students:** Learning about biology, ecology, and sustainable agriculture.
* **Volunteers:** Gaining practical gardening skills while giving back to the community.

<img src="{{ images.student.url }}" width="{{ images.student.width }}" height="{{ images.student.height }}" alt="{{ images.student.alt }}" loading="lazy" class="block full-width" crossorigin="anonymous" referrerpolicy="no-referrer" />

## 🎭 Community Event Space
Beyond the rows of crops, the garden is a vibrant venue for community gatherings. It provides a serene, outdoor setting for events that bring the Kern River Valley together.
