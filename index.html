KRV Bridge Connection

The KRV Bridge Connection is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to strengthening the Kern River Valley community. Our mission is to bridge individuals and families to services that address food insecurity, homelessness, and the needs of low-income households.

KRVBC is a grassroots hub of united public, private, and non-profit sectors, bridging our underserved with locally accessible advocates and resources to uplift our community as a whole.

Community Involvement

The KRV Bridge Connection is built on community participation. Our work is strengthened by individuals, families, and local partners coming together to support one another and improve access to essential resources across the Kern River Valley.

Community events create opportunities to connect, share resources, and support one another through meaningful, local engagement.
